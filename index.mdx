## Get started

<Steps>
  <Step title="Sign in to Fire242">
    Navigate to the Fire242 login page and enter your credentials. Your administrator will provide your username and temporary password.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Explore your dashboard">
    After signing in you'll land on the dashboard for your role — Admin, Manager, or Canvasser. The dashboard shows live statistics relevant to your responsibilities.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Find voters in your constituency">
    Use the voter search to look up residents by name, address, or voter ID. Review their party affiliation, registration status, and past survey history.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Conduct your first survey">
    Open the Survey form, search for the voter, and step through the questions. Submit the completed survey — results appear in your team's reports immediately.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Note>
  Your access level depends on the role assigned to your account. See [User Roles](/roles/overview) for a full breakdown of what each role can see and do.
</Note>
