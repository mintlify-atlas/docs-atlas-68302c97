---

Fire242 gives political organizations a single platform to manage every part of a voter outreach operation — from maintaining the voter database to coordinating door-to-door canvassing, collecting exit polls, and analyzing results on election day.

<Note>
  Your access to features depends on the role assigned to your account. Fire242 has three roles: Admin, Manager, and Canvasser. See [User roles](/roles/overview) for a full breakdown.
</Note>

## Key capabilities

**Voter database** — Maintain a comprehensive, searchable voter registry with party affiliation, registration status, polling division, address details, and complete contact history.

**Voter surveys** — Canvassers use a structured, multi-step survey form to record voter responses in the field. Survey results are immediately visible in reports.

**Geo-fencing** — Visualize constituency boundaries on an interactive map. Admins define geo-fence areas; the platform uses them to assign and validate canvasser locations.

**Exit polls** — Collect and track real-time exit poll data on polling day, broken down by constituency and polling station.

**Reporting** — Admins and Managers access dashboards with live statistics: surveyed voter counts, quota progress by canvasser, polling-day turnout graphs, and full election reports.

**User management** — Admins create and manage accounts for Managers and Canvassers, assign constituency access, configure permissions, and control operating hours.

## Who uses Fire242

<CardGroup cols={3}>
  <Card title="Admin" icon="shield-halved" href="/roles/admin">
    Full platform access. Creates users, manages voter data, configures constituencies and geo-fences, and views all reports across the entire operation.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Manager" icon="users-gear" href="/roles/manager">
    Oversees canvassers within assigned constituencies. Reviews survey results, monitors quota progress, manages exit polls, and adjusts system settings for their team.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Canvasser" icon="person-walking-arrow-right" href="/roles/canvasser">
    Conducts voter surveys in the field. Searches the voter database, submits survey responses, views their assigned quota, and checks polling results.
  </Card>
</CardGroup>

<Tip>
  Canvassers are referred to as `user` in the platform's underlying role system. When you see role-based routing to `/panel/user/dashboard`, that refers to the Canvasser role.
</Tip>

## Platform sections

Fire242 is organized into role-specific panels:

| Panel path | Who uses it | Purpose |
|---|---|---|
| `/panel/admin/dashboard` | Admin | Full platform overview and management |
| `/panel/manager/dashboard` | Manager | Constituency-scoped oversight |
| `/panel/user/dashboard` | Canvasser | Survey work and quota tracking |

After signing in, you are automatically redirected to the dashboard for your role.
