---
title: "Quick start"
description: "Sign in to Fire242 and complete your first voter survey in a few steps."
---

This guide walks you through signing in, orienting yourself on the dashboard, finding a voter, conducting a survey, and viewing the results. The steps apply to the Canvasser (user) role, which is the most common starting point for new users.

<Info>
  Admins and Managers follow the same sign-in process but land on different dashboards with different features available. See [User roles](/roles/overview) for role-specific details.
</Info>

<Steps>
  <Step title="Sign in to Fire242">
    Navigate to the Fire242 login page. Enter your **email address** and **password**, then click **Sign In**.

    If you have a valid session from a previous visit, the platform automatically redirects you to your role dashboard — you won't see the login form again until your session expires.

    <Warning>
      Fire242 accounts are created by an Admin. If you don't have credentials yet, contact your administrator to have an account set up for you.
    </Warning>

    On successful sign-in, you are redirected to your role dashboard:
    - Admins go to `/panel/admin/dashboard`
    - Managers go to `/panel/manager/dashboard`
    - Canvassers go to `/panel/user/dashboard`
  </Step>

  <Step title="Explore your dashboard">
    Your dashboard shows live statistics relevant to your role. For Canvassers, this includes your surveyed voter count, your assigned quota, and recent activity.

    Use the sidebar navigation to move between sections:
    - **Voters** — search and view voter records
    - **Survey** — open the voter survey form
    - **Polling result** — view polling-day results for your constituency
    - **View quota** — check your surveying targets and progress

    <Tip>
      Check your quota on the **View quota** page before heading out. It shows how many voters you are expected to survey and how many you have completed.
    </Tip>
  </Step>

  <Step title="Find a voter">
    Navigate to **Voters** in the sidebar. Use the search to locate the voter you want to survey.

    You can search by:
    - **Name** — enter a first name, last name, or both
    - **Voter ID** — enter the voter's registration number
    - **Address** — enter a street address or house number
    - **National Registry** — search the national voter registry by name or ID

    Results appear as you type. Select the voter from the suggestions to open their full record.

    The voter record shows their party affiliation, registration status, polling division, address, and any prior survey responses.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Conduct a survey">
    From the voter's record, click the survey action to open the survey form, or navigate to **Survey** in the sidebar and search for the voter there.

    The survey form steps through a set of questions configured by your Admin or Manager. For each question, select or enter the voter's response.

    When you reach the final step, review the responses and click **Submit** to save the survey.

    <Note>
      Survey questions are set up by Admins and Managers. If you see unexpected questions or options, contact your Manager to review the survey configuration.
    </Note>
  </Step>

  <Step title="View your results">
    After submitting a survey, you can confirm it was recorded:

    - Your **dashboard** updates the surveyed voter count immediately.
    - The **Voters** list shows a surveyed indicator on the voter's record.
    - The voter's survey history shows the response you just submitted.

    Managers and Admins see your submissions aggregated in their reports and can view individual survey detail through the admin panel.
  </Step>
</Steps>
